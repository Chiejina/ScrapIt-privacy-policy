Privacy Policy for Scrap It

Last Updated: August 2, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Scrap It ("we," "our," or "us")! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application Scrap It (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

User Content: We collect and store the content you create, upload, or share through our App, including photos, puzzle configurations, and scrapbook elements.

Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and other contact information.

Communications: If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you, such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our App, including hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with the App, such as the features you use, the time and duration of your activities, and other usage patterns.

Photo Library Access: With your permission, we access your device's photo library to allow you to select images for creating puzzles and scrapbooks. We do not copy or store these photos on our servers unless you explicitly save them as part of your projects.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Develop new products and features
  • Personalize your experience
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Protect the rights and property of Scrap It and others

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information about you in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: We may share or transfer your information in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, dissolution, transaction, or proceeding involving the sale, transfer, or divestiture of all or a portion of our business.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with your consent or at your direction.

5. Advertising and Analytics

We may allow third parties to provide analytics services and serve advertisements on our behalf. These entities may use cookies, web beacons, and other technologies to collect information about your use of the App, such as your IP address, device identifiers, and advertising identifiers. This information may be used by us and others to, among other things, analyze and track data, determine the popularity of certain content, deliver advertising targeted to your interests, and better understand your online activity.

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements within our App. Google AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising. To learn more about how Google uses your data, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

6. Your Choices

Account Information: You may update or correct information about yourself by managing your account settings within the App or by contacting us.

Photo Library Access: You can control our access to your device's photo library through your device settings.

Promotional Communications: You may opt out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the instructions in those emails. If you opt out, we may still send you non-promotional communications, such as those about your account or our ongoing business relations.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the security of our systems.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or by other means, such as email. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the App to stay informed about our information practices.
